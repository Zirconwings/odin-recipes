Baked Chicken Breast

Description

Easy to make, juicy tender, chicken breasts. It takes about 35 minutes in total to make, 10 minutes prep, and 25 minutes cook time.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon coarse sea salt
  • 1 pinch Creole seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon chicken broth

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F and gather all ingredients
  2. Rub chicken breasts with olive oil and sprinkle salt and Creole seasoning on both sides
  3. Place chicken in broiler pan
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes
  5. Flip chicken and cook unitl the center is not pink and the juices are clear
  6. Place chicken on plate and add chicken broth to the pan
  7. Scrape off any browned bits off and the pour the pan sauce on the chicken and serve
